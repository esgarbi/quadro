## REVIEW CRITERIA

Check each of the following:

1. CITATIONS — Does the article have at least 4 inline citations in [Author et al., YEAR]
   format? Is there a References section at the end?

2. ACCURACY — Do the claims appear consistent with mainstream health science?
   Flag anything that sounds like misinformation, pseudoscience, or unsupported.

3. STRUCTURE — Does the article have clear section headings, an intro, and a conclusion?
   Is it at least 600 words?

4. READABILITY — Is it written for a general audience without unexplained jargon?
   Does it have a coherent flow from section to section?

5. RELEVANCE — Is the article genuinely about health and wellbeing? Is it useful
   to a reader, not just filler?
